# Chapter Expansion Instructions

Use this checklist to bring a draft chapter up to the encyclopedia's complete
topic-map and step-by-step lab standard before it is marked publication-ready.

1. Confirm every section in [chapter.md](chapter.md) is present and populated
   — no placeholder text remains.
2. Expand the theory section until every term used later in the chapter (and
   in the volume glossary) is introduced and explained.
3. Verify implementation steps are complete enough to execute without
   consulting outside material, and that CLI/code examples are consistent
   with the versions recorded in [SOFTWARE_VERSIONS.md](../SOFTWARE_VERSIONS.md).
4. Add a negative test case to the hands-on lab where practical (a
   misconfiguration, a failure injection, or an expected error).
5. Cross-check that every new term appears in the volume
   `GLOSSARY.md` and every chapter title is linked from the volume
   `README.md` and `INDEX.md`.
6. Run the [technical-review checklist](technical-review-checklist.md) before
   advancing the chapter to publication.
