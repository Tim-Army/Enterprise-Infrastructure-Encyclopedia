# Technical Review Checklist

Complete before marking a chapter as published.

- [ ] All chapter framework sections are present and complete.
- [ ] Learning objectives are verifiable and match the chapter body.
- [ ] Commands, APIs, and version numbers match [SOFTWARE_VERSIONS.md](../SOFTWARE_VERSIONS.md).
- [ ] The hands-on lab is reproducible from a clean environment, including
      cleanup steps.
- [ ] Security guidance reflects current vendor best practice for the topic.
- [ ] New terms are defined in the volume glossary.
- [ ] Internal links (volume README, INDEX, MASTER_TOC) resolve correctly.
- [ ] Editorial standards in [EDITORIAL_STANDARDS.md](../EDITORIAL_STANDARDS.md)
      are met (tone, formatting, heading levels).
- [ ] No proprietary exam questions or licensed vendor material are reproduced.
