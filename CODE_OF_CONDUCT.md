# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want participation in this project to be a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in reviews, issues, and discussions.
- Focus feedback on the content, not the contributor.
- Assume good faith; ask clarifying questions before assuming intent.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or reviews.

## Enforcement

Report concerns by opening an issue marked confidential or contacting the
repository owner directly. Reports will be reviewed and addressed promptly;
maintainers may remove content or restrict participation in response to
violations.
