# Chapter 10: Appendix — Cisco U. Learning Paths and Continuing Education Credits

[Cisco U.](https://u.cisco.com) is Cisco's official learning platform. Its
**Learning Paths** are the unit that carries **Continuing Education (CE)
credits** — the credits an active Cisco certification holder can apply toward
recertification instead of retaking an exam. This appendix lists every
Learning Path in the public Cisco U. catalog with the **exam it prepares you
for** (where one maps), its CE-credit value, and whether it is **Free**
(available with a no-cost Cisco U. account) or needs a paid **Subscription**.

**Snapshot — 22 July 2026:** 215 Learning Paths; 43 map to a specific Cisco exam and
5 more to a certification without a listed exam; 108 carry CE credits; 22 are free and
193 require a subscription. Some tracks hosted on Cisco U. cover non-Cisco technologies
(AWS, Azure, Microsoft, Kubernetes, and others) and carry no CE credits or exam.

> **On the Exam / cert column.** Most values come from Cisco U.'s own catalog
> tagging. Five courses the catalog left untagged or stale-tagged are filled from
> verified current sources: **350-101 WLCOR** (wireless core), **300-110 WLSD** and
> **300-120 WLSI** (wireless design and implementation), **300-620 DCACI** (ACI), and
> **300-710 SNCF** (advanced firewall). Cisco split wireless into its own **CCNP
> Wireless** track on 19 March 2026 — WLCOR replaced ENCOR as its core, and WLSD/WLSI
> are the realigned successors of the retired 300-425 ENWLSD and 300-430 ENWLSI that
> the two "Enterprise Wireless" paths were built for. A blank ("—") means no current
> exam maps; the Collaboration Applications/Conferencing courses stay blank because
> their concentration exams (e.g., CLICA 300-810) are retiring in 2026. **Currency:**
> catalog contents, credit values, exam codes, and access change often; verify on each
> path's page and at [ce.cisco.com](https://ce.cisco.com) before relying on a value.
