# Chapter 04: Appendix — Dell Technologies Certifications and Course Access

Every current Dell Technologies Proven Professional certification with
its exam code, its official page, and how its training is accessed.
The certification list was verified against Dell Learning's
certification catalog on **22 July 2026** — the same harvest that
anchors
[Volume XXXII — Dell Technologies Certification Tracks](../../volume-32-dell-technologies-certifications/README.md).

**How access works at Dell.** Unlike Cisco U. or Juniper Open
Learning, Dell's course catalog sits behind a Dell Learning login and
does not expose per-course pricing publicly, so this appendix records
the program's access model rather than per-course labels: formal
preparation courses are **paid** — via the Dell Learning Hub
subscription, on-demand purchases, or instructor-led training named
on each exam's catalog entry — while each exam's **description
document (objectives, weights, recommended training) is free**, as
are practice-test information pages. Exams themselves are purchased
through Pearson VUE. Where Dell runs free-training promotions, the
exam page below is the place they appear.

> **Currency.** This program rolls exam series faster than any other
> on this shelf (-23 → -01 → -A-00 within two years). Re-verify the
> code on its exam page the week you book.
