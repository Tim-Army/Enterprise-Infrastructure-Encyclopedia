# Chapter 05: Appendix — Palo Alto Networks Certifications and Course Access

Every current Palo Alto Networks certification, its official page, and
how its training is accessed. The certification list was verified
against Palo Alto Networks' certification pages on **22 July 2026** —
the same harvest that extended
[Volume XVI — Palo Alto Networks Security](../../volume-16-palo-alto-networks-security/README.md)
with the Security Operations and Cloud Security tracks.

**How access works at Palo Alto Networks.** In 2025 Palo Alto Networks
replaced its exam-coded certifications (PCNSA, PCNSE, PCDRA, and peers)
with a **role-based program** — named credentials across three tracks,
no exam codes. Preparation for every certification is delivered as
**free digital learning** through Palo Alto Networks' **Beacon**
learning platform, with **optional paid instructor-led training (ILT)**
for deeper, hands-on preparation. Each certification page below links
its recommended digital-learning path and its exam registration.

> **Currency.** Palo Alto Networks reshaped this program rapidly during
> 2025-2026 (the XDR Analyst/Engineer pair launched 29 April 2025,
> Security Operations Professional 30 May 2025). Confirm the
> certification and its recommended training on its page before
> starting.
