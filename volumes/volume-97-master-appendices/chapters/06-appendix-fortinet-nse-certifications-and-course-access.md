# Chapter 06: Appendix — Fortinet NSE Certifications and Course Access

Every certification in Fortinet's NSE program as restored on **15 July
2026**, organized by track and level, with the training-access model.
The certification lineup and level mappings were verified against
Fortinet Training Institute's certification pages and the official 2026
program-change transition mapping on **22 July 2026** — the same
harvest that anchors
[Volume XXXIII — Fortinet NSE Program](../../volume-33-fortinet-nse-program/README.md)
and complements
[Volume XIX — Fortinet Network Security](../../volume-19-fortinet-network-security/README.md)
(NSE 1–4).

**How access works at Fortinet.** Fortinet's model is unusually open:
the **self-paced NSE training courses and many hands-on labs are free**
through the Fortinet Training Institute (free account) — this is how
most candidates prepare for NSE 1–7. **Instructor-led training (ILT)**
is the paid, deeper option. **Exams** are delivered by Pearson VUE
(fees apply, except the free fundamentals levels); the NSE 8 practical
is a paid, proctored lab. Each certification's page on the Fortinet
Training Institute links its free course and its exam registration.

> **Currency.** This program was renamed twice in three years (NSE →
> FCP/FCSS/FCX → NSE, restored 15 July 2026). Confirm the exam name,
> level, and its recommended (free) course on the Fortinet Training
> Institute page before starting.

## Free training

- **Fortinet Training Institute self-paced NSE courses and labs**
  (free, account required) — the recommended preparation for NSE 1–7
- **Free exams** at the fundamentals levels (NSE 1–3); technical-level
  exams (NSE 4+) carry Pearson VUE fees
