# Chapter 08: Appendix — AWS Certifications and Course Access

Every current AWS certification, organized by level, with its exam code,
any announced exam end date, and the official training that prepares it.
Every code and date below was harvested on **23 July 2026** from AWS's own
certification pages — read from the AWS Skill Builder exam-prep link each
certification page carries, because AWS no longer prints the exam code in
page body text — the same harvest that anchors
[Volume XVII — AWS Architecture and Security](../../volume-17-aws-architecture-security/README.md),
whose Chapters 10–13 map this program to hands-on content.

**How access works at AWS.** AWS's model is unusually generous at the
training layer and strict at the exam layer. **[AWS Skill Builder](https://skillbuilder.aws/)**
carries a **free tier** that includes a structured **exam-prep plan for
every certification** — the plans linked in the tables below — alongside
hundreds of digital courses; a paid individual subscription adds official
practice exams, AWS Cloud Quest, and hands-on labs. **Classroom and
instructor-led training** through AWS Training Partners is paid.
**Exams** are always paid, booked through your AWS Certification Account
and delivered by AWS's authorized testing partners, at a test center or
online-proctored. Passing any exam earns a **50% discount voucher** toward
your next one.

> **Currency.** This program reorganizes faster than most. Within one
> generation: four specialty certifications retired, two role-based
> associates were added, SysOps Administrator was renamed **CloudOps
> Engineer**, a **Generative AI Developer – Professional** was introduced,
> **Advanced Networking – Specialty retires 25 August 2026**, and
> **Machine Learning Engineer moves MLA-C01 → MLA-C02** with registration
> opening 1 September 2026. Confirm any code and status on the
> certification's own AWS page before registering or purchasing. Volume
> XVII, Chapter 13 defines the recurring currency check that keeps this
> table honest.

## Free training and entry points

- **[AWS Skill Builder free tier](https://skillbuilder.aws/)** — a free
  exam-prep plan for every certification below, plus digital courses;
  account required, no payment
- **[AWS Certification](https://aws.amazon.com/certification/)** — official
  exam guides (free downloads) and the authoritative lineup
- **AWS Free Tier** — the practical lab substrate for hands-on preparation;
  pair it with a budget alarm as Volume XVII, Chapter 10 sets up
- **50% retake/next-exam discount voucher** — earned by passing any exam,
  claimed from your AWS Certification Account
- Everything marked **paid** below is a Skill Builder subscription,
  official practice exam, or partner-delivered classroom training

## Reading the exam codes

The three-letter prefix identifies the certification; the **`-C0x` suffix
is the blueprint version**, incrementing when AWS rebuilds the exam. A
resource written for `SAA-C02` or `SCS-C02` is a blueprint behind, and the
difference is real content rather than cosmetics. Check the suffix before
buying anything.

## Notes on validity and cost

- Every AWS certification is valid for **three years**. Earning a
  higher-level certification generally extends the validity of lower-level
  ones you hold — confirm the current rule on AWS's recertification page,
  since this policy has been revised before.
- Exam pricing is published at registration in your AWS Certification
  Account rather than on the certification pages, and varies by level and
  region; confirm it there rather than from any summary.
- Practice exams and hands-on labs sit behind the paid Skill Builder
  subscription; the **exam-prep plans linked above are free**, which makes
  them the correct starting point for every certification in this appendix.
