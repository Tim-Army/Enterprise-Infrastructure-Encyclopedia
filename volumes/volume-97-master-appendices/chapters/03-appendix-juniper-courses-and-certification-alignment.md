# Chapter 03: Appendix — Juniper Courses and Certification Alignment

Juniper's official training for every current certification exam, with
the exam each course prepares you for and whether it is free. The
course-to-exam mapping is copied from the **Recommended Training**
section of each exam's page on Juniper's certification site — the same
pages that anchor
[Volume XXXI — Juniper Networks Certification Tracks](../../volume-31-juniper-networks-certifications/README.md)
— so every row is primary-source verified; nothing is inferred.

**Snapshot — 22 July 2026.** Access labels reflect Juniper's **Open
Learning** program, which provides the associate-level preparation
courses free (with discounted exam vouchers) through a no-cost
Learning Portal login; Open Learning also covers three
specialist-level certifications — confirm current coverage on the
portal. Specialist and professional courses are paid
(instructor-led or on-demand), as are the JNCIE self-study bundles.
The portal shows prices at enrollment; course pages require login.

> **Currency.** Juniper refreshes exam codes and course lineups
> frequently (JN0-106 replaced JN0-105 in April 2026; JPR-946 arrived
> in July 2026). Re-verify the code and its recommended training on
> the exam's Juniper page before registering or purchasing.

## Free entry points

- **Juniper Open Learning** — free certification prep with exam-voucher
  discounts, via the [Juniper Learning Portal](https://learningportal.juniper.net/)
- **Juniper vLabs** and the free **vJunos** images — every written
  exam's objectives are practicable at no cost (Volume XXXI, Chapter 01)
- **Day One books** — Juniper's free technical library
