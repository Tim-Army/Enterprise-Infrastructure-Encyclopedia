# Volume XCVII Glossary

Definitions for terms used in **Volume XCVII — Master Appendices**,
alphabetized. See also the [volume index](INDEX.md) and the
[master glossary](../../GLOSSARY.md) for cross-volume terminology.

**Appendix** — Supplementary reference material — a data table, catalog,
or as-built record — that supports a volume without belonging to its
chapter narrative. In this encyclopedia every appendix lives in Volume
XCVII, with a pointer left in its source volume.

**CE (Continuing Education) credits** — Credits an active Cisco
certification holder earns from qualifying training and applies toward
recertification instead of retaking an exam; submitted at ce.cisco.com.
Used in Chapter 01.

**Prefix length** — The IPv6 notation (for example `/64`) for the size
of a network, used where IPv4 writes a dotted network mask. Used in
Chapter 02.
