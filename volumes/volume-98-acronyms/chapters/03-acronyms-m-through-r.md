# Chapter 03: Acronyms M through R

Every acronym and initialism in this range that the encyclopedia uses,
with what it stands for. An entry appears here only when the shelf uses
it at least three times and its expansion is either taken from the
volume that introduces it or is the standard, unambiguous industry
expansion; where an acronym has more than one meaning on this shelf,
the entry lists each. For fuller definitions, follow the term into the
glossary of the volume that introduces it.
