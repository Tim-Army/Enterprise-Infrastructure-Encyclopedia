# Volume XCVIII Glossary

Definitions for terms used in **Volume XCVIII — Acronyms**,
alphabetized. See also the [volume index](INDEX.md) and the
[master glossary](../../GLOSSARY.md) for cross-volume terminology.

**Acronym** — An abbreviation formed from the initial letters or parts
of a phrase, pronounced as a word (RAID, CAPWAP) or letter by letter.
This volume uses the term loosely to cover both acronyms proper and
initialisms.

**Expansion** — The phrase an acronym stands for. This volume records
expansions only; conceptual definitions live in the glossary of the
volume that introduces each term.

**Initialism** — An abbreviation pronounced letter by letter rather
than as a word (BGP, DNS, SSH).
